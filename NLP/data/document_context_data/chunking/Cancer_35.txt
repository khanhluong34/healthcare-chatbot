Cancer Cancer A low-carbohydrate diet  has sometimes been recommended as a supportive therapy for cancer treatment. Most cancers are initially recognized either because of the appearance of signs or symptoms or through screening.  Neither of these leads to a definitive diagnosis, which requires the examination of a tissue sample by a pathologist.  People with suspected cancer are investigated with medical tests.  These commonly include blood tests, X-rays,  CT scans and endoscopy. The tissue diagnosis from the biopsy indicates the type of cell that is proliferating, its histological grade, genetic abnormalities and other features.  Together, this information is useful to evaluate the prognosis and to choose the best treatment Cancer Cancer
