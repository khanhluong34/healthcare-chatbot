Mental illness Mental illness  Some examples include autism spectrum disorder, oppositional defiant disorder and conduct disorder, and attention deficit hyperactivity disorder , which may continue into adulthood.  Conduct disorder, if continuing into adulthood, may be diagnosed as antisocial personality disorder .  Popular labels such as psychopath  do not appear in the DSM or ICD but are linked by some to these diagnoses. Somatoform disorders may be diagnosed when there are problems that appear to originate in the body that are thought to be manifestations of a mental disorder.  This includes somatization disorder and conversion disorder.  There are also disorders of how a person perceives their body, such as body dysmorphic disorder.
