Diabetes Diabetes  In both species, all breeds may be affected, but some small dog breeds are particularly likely to develop diabetes, such as Miniature Poodles. Feline diabetes is strikingly similar to human type 2 diabetes.  The Burmese, Russian Blue, Abyssinian, and Norwegian Forest cat breeds are at higher risk than other breeds.  Overweight cats are also at higher risk. The symptoms may relate to fluid loss and polyuria, but the course may also be insidious.  Diabetic animals are more prone to infections.
