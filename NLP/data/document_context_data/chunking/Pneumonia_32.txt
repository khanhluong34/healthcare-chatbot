Pneumonia Pneumonia  In children those with respiratory distress or oxygen saturations of less than 90% should be hospitalized.  The utility of chest physiotherapy in pneumonia has not yet been determined.  Over-the-counter cough medicine has not been found to be effective, nor has the use of zinc supplementation in children.  There is insufficient evidence for mucolytics.  There is no strong evidence to recommend that children who have non-measles related pneumonia take vitamin A supplements.  Vitamin D, as of 2018 is of unclear benefit in children.
