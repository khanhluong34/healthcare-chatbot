Mental illness Mental illness  Impulse-control disorders and a few anxiety disorders tend to appear in childhood.  Some other anxiety disorders, substance disorders, and mood disorders emerge later in the mid-teens.  Symptoms of schizophrenia typically manifest from late adolescence to early twenties. The likely course and outcome of mental disorders vary and are dependent on numerous factors related to the disorder itself, the individual as a whole, and the social environment.  Some disorders may last a brief period of time, while others may be long-term in nature. All disorders can have a varied course.
