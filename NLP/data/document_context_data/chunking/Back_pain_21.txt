Although back pain does not typically cause permanent disability, it is a significant contributor to physician visits and missed work days in the United States, and is the single leading cause of disability worldwide.  The American Academy of Orthopaedic Surgeons report approximately 12 million visits to doctor's offices each year are due to back pain.  Missed work and disability related to low back pain costs over $50 billion each year in the United States.
