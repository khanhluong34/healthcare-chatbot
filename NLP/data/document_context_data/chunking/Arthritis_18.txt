People with hand arthritis can have trouble with simple activities of daily living tasks , such as turning a key in a lock or opening jars, as these activities can be cumbersome and painful.  There are adaptive aids or assistive devices  available to help with these tasks, but they are generally more costly than conventional products with the same function.  It is now possible to 3-D print adaptive aids, which have been released as open source hardware to reduce patient costs.
