Diabetes Diabetes  Anti-diabetic medications such as metformin and semaglutide, as well as lifestyle modifications, can be used to prevent or respond to type 2 diabetes.  Gestational diabetes normally resolves shortly after delivery. As of 2019, an estimated 463 million people had diabetes worldwide accounting for 8. 8% of the adult population.  Type 2 diabetes makes up about 90% of all diabetes cases.  The prevalence of the disease continues to increase, most dramatically in low- and middle-income nations.
