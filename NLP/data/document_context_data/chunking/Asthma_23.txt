Asthma Asthma Aspirin-exacerbated respiratory disease , also known as aspirin-induced asthma, affects up to 9% of asthmatics.  AERD consists of asthma, nasal polyps, sinus disease, and respiratory reactions to aspirin and other NSAID medications .  People often also develop loss of smell and most experience respiratory reactions to alcohol. Alcohol may worsen asthmatic symptoms in up to a third of people.  This may be even more common in some ethnic groups such as the Japanese and those with aspirin-exacerbated respiratory disease.
