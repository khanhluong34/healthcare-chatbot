The predominant view as of 2018 is that genetic, psychological, and environmental factors all contribute to the development or progression of mental disorders.  Different risk factors may be present at different ages, with risk occurring as early as during prenatal period. A number of psychiatric disorders are linked to a family history .  Twin studies have also revealed a very high heritability for many mental disorders .
