Obesity Obesity  Calorie count laws and nutrition facts labels attempt to steer people toward making healthier food choices, including awareness of how much food energy is being consumed. Obese people consistently under-report their food consumption as compared to people of normal weight.  This is supported both by tests of people carried out in a calorimeter room and by direct observation. A sedentary lifestyle may play a significant role in obesity.  Worldwide there has been a large shift towards less physically demanding work, and currently at least 30% of the world's population gets insufficient exercise.  This is primarily due to increasing use of mechanized transportation and a greater prevalence of labor-saving technology in the home.  In children, there appear to be declines in levels of physical activity , likely due to safety concerns, changes in social interaction , and inadequate urban design  Obesity Obesity
