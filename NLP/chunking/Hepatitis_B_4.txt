Achieving this goal would require the development of therapeutic treatments to cure chronic hepatitis B, as well as preventing its transmission and using vaccines to prevent new infections. An estimated 296 million people, or 3. 8% of the global population, had chronic hepatitis B infections as of 2019.  Another 1. 5 million developed acute infections that year, and 820,000 deaths occurred as a result of HBV.
