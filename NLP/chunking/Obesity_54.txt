Obesity Obesity  In 2022 the National Institute for Health and Care Research  published a comprehensive review of research on what local authorities can do to reduce obesity. The Obesity Policy Action  framework divides measure into upstream policies, midstream policies, and downstream policies.  Upstream policies have to do with changing society, while midstream policies try to alter behaviors believed to contribute to obesity at the individual level, while downstream policies treat currently obese people. The healthy BMI range varies with the age and sex of the child.  Obesity in children and adolescents is defined as a BMI greater than the 95th percentile.  The reference data that these percentiles are based on is from 1963 to 1994 and thus has not been affected by the recent increases in rates of obesity.
