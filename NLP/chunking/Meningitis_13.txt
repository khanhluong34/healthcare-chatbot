The meninges comprise three membranes that, together with the cerebrospinal fluid, enclose and protect the brain and spinal cord .  The pia mater is a delicate impermeable membrane that firmly adheres to the surface of the brain, following all the minor contours.  The arachnoid mater  is a loosely fitting sac on top of the pia mater.
