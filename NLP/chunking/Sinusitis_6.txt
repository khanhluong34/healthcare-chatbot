Sinusitis Sinusitis  Often, chronic sinusitis can lead to anosmia, the loss of the sense of smell. The four paired paranasal sinuses are the frontal, ethmoidal, maxillary, and sphenoidal sinuses.  The ethmoidal sinuses are further subdivided into anterior and posterior ethmoid sinuses, the division of which is defined as the basal lamella of the middle nasal concha.
