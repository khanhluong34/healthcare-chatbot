Encephalitis Encephalitis  Herpes simplex encephalitis has an incidence of 2–4 per million of the population per year. Encephalitis with meningitis is known as meningoencephalitis, while encephalitis with involvement of the spinal cord is known as encephalomyelitis. The word is from Ancient Greek ἐγκέφαλος, enképhalos "brain", composed of ἐν, en, "in" and κεφαλή, kephalé, "head", and the medical suffix -itis "inflammation".  Encephalitis Encephalitis
