Obesity Obesity Smoking has a significant effect on an individual's weight.  Those who quit smoking gain an average of 4. 4 kilograms  for men and 5. 0 kilograms  for women over ten years.  However, changing rates of smoking have had little effect on the overall rates of obesity. In the United States, the number of children a person has is related to their risk of obesity.
