Diabetes Diabetes  Home telehealth support can be an effective management technique. The use of technology to deliver educational programs for adults with type 2 diabetes includes computer-based self-management interventions to collect for tailored responses to facilitate self-management.  There is no adequate evidence to support effects on cholesterol, blood pressure, behavioral change , depression, weight and health-related quality of life, nor in other biological, cognitive or emotional outcomes. In 2017, 425 million people had diabetes worldwide, up from an estimated 382 million people in 2013 and from 108 million in 1980.
