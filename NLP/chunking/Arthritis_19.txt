Low level laser therapy may be considered for relief of pain and stiffness associated with arthritis.  Evidence of benefit is tentative. Pulsed electromagnetic field therapy  has tentative evidence supporting improved functioning but no evidence of improved pain in osteoarthritis.  The FDA has not approved PEMFT for the treatment of arthritis.  In Canada, PEMF devices are legally licensed by Health Canada for the treatment of pain associated with arthritic conditions.
