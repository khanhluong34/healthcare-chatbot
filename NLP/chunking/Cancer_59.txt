The three most common childhood cancers are leukemia , brain tumors  and lymphomas .  In the United States cancer affects about 1 in 285 children.  Rates of childhood cancer increased by 0. 6% per year between 1975 and 2002 in the United States and by 1. 1% per year between 1978 and 1997 in Europe.
